CHILDHOOD

Itachi was the first child born to Mikoto and Fugaku Uchiha.
His early childhood was marked with violence:
4600when he was four years old,the Third Shinobi World War
waged and he witnessed first-hand many of the war's
casualties. The death and destruction he experienced
at such a young age traumatised Itachi and made him a pacifist,
leading to him training non-stop to achieve his dream
of becoming a ninja in order to erase fighting from the world.

Izumi first met Itachi several years earlier than in the novel,
when she invited him to play with her and other children
their age. He declined, prompting the other children
to start bullying him.Izumi alone stood up for him.
Izumi graduated from the Academy at age eleven,a year early

and thus allowed her to spend more time with Itachi. While they were at a tea shop,Izumi
told him that she wanted to become a strong ninja and protect others from hate.but
Itachi hurt her feelings by criticizing her.

Shisui came to care for Itachi very much; in fact, he was
like a big brother to Itachi. Shisui taught Itachi that
"self-sacrifice… a nameless shinobi who protects peace
from within its shadow…" was the true mark of a shinobi
At age 5, after becoming a big brother to Sasuke,
Itachi was approached by Shisui, who offered to train together.
Soon, the two became best friends, forming a brother-like bond
as they continued to teach the other new tricks....

Immediately after the war ended a year later, Shisui witnessed
his friend die on a mission, consequently awakening the
Mangekyō Sharingan as he felt responsible. A year later,
he met a 5 year old Itachi Uchiha, whom he became
best friends with while the two formed a brother-like bond.


Itachi distances himself from his clan, as some clan members
believed he murdered his best friend, Shisui Uchiha,
to gain the Mangekyo Sharingan........









































































THE SACRIFICE

Since the Hidden Leaf Village's founding days, many were suspicious of
the Uchiha clan,most of all Tobirama Senju, the second Hokage,
who wasn't nearly as trusting or idealistic as his brother Hashirama.
Tobirama's suspicions were largely justified, too, in light of
Madara Uchiha's revolt against the Leaf Village, which continued to
spark fear and mistrust in the hearts of many. The nine-tailed fox
attack further cemented people's fears of the Uchiha clan,
and the village advisors became wary.

With fears mounting, the village leaders decided to restrict the Uchiha
to the village's outskirts. This exclusion infuriated the Uchiha.
Itachi's father Fugaku, the Uchiha clan head, entered his son into
the Anbu black ops, ordering him to spy on the village leaders and
the other clans to open an opportunity for them to attempt a coup.
At this point, however, Itachi is already a spy for the village advisor
Danzo Shimura. With his double-agent status, Itachi believed that
he was aiding the cause of peace. Danzo believed that
if left unchecked,the Uchihas would incite another war.

Itachi informed Danzo of his family's coup plans, and Danzo manipulated
Itachi into believing that coup could not be stopped without bloodshed.
Danzo also stole Shisui Uchiha's eye, which led Itachi to become

conflicted about whom he could trust. Ultimately, Danzo played on Itachi's trauma and naïveté
to manipulate him into killing the Uchiha clan. By doing this, Itachi had hopes that eliminating
those few causing tension in the village would potentially help them to avoid another Shinobi
war and the loss of many innocent lives.

In order to set Sasuke on road he cast himself as the villain,
by allowing Sasuke to find him standing over their parents,
using Tsukuyomi to him with visions of their murders.
Sasuke fled, fearing his for his life, but Itachi chased him
down to divulge the lie he had concocted: he killed their
family to test his mettle and now wanted to test himself
againstthe only remaining challenge to him,
the little brother who he not loved.........

He encouraged Sasuke to become strong enough to kill him
and take revenge,and to that end suggested he acquire
Mangekyō Sharingan of his own.

Itachi left, but was surprised to find Sasuke was pursuing him with a newly-awakened Sharingan.
Sasuke's attempt was failed and he passed out, but not before seeing Itachi cry with remorse.

REANIMATION

Itachi was reanimated by Kabuto during the Fourth Great
Ninja War to fight for him, but he was ultimately able to
break through his control and fought against him instead.
In an intense fight, Itachi proved to be successful in
stopping Kabuto Yakushi, thanks to the usage of the
forbidden jutsu, Izanami But when Itachi was alive,
he couldn't maintain his full potential due to the fact that
he didn't had an eternal mangekyo sharingan and great
chakra reserves, while when reanimated he attains
unlimited stamina and chakra hence curing his weak points.
So, Yes reanimated Itachi is the strongest state of ITACHI.

Itachi Uchiha breaks free from the Reanimation Jutsu
through his own willpower and the actions of sasuke.
During the Fourth Great Ninja War, Kabuto Yakushi
reanimated Itachi to use him as a tool in battle against
the Allied Shinobi Forces.

Itachi's strong will and love for his brother, Sasuke, allowed him to resist Kabuto's control
to some extent. Itachi also left behind a crow with a portion of his consciousness and
the Amaterasu technique sealed within it. This crow was implanted in Naruto Uzumaki
by Itachi before his death.During the battle, Sasuke is able to confront Itachi
within the Reanimation Jutsu and has a heartfelt conversation with him.
This emotional connection between the brothers helps Itachi break free from
Kabuto's control. Itachi then uses the crow within Naruto to trap Kabuto in a genjutsu,
releasing the Reanimation Jutsu's hold over him,allowing him to finally rest in peace.
